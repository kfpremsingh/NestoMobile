FESTIVE DRIVE

  • On a touch device you can use pinch to zoom, drag to move the zoomed image, or swipe to cycle through images.
  • On a PC you can use mousescroll to zoom, drag to move the zoomed image or cycle through images.
  • Be creative with your image description. Add color, links or even images
  • TouchNSwipe works on any window size. Resize your window to see it work.
  • TouchNSwipe has tons of settings. Create your own look or build from starter templates.
